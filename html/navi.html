Natus Vincere

On this page you will get some Information about the current No. 1 CSGO-Team in the World.

History of Natus Vincere

Natus Vincere (latin "Born to win" and often abbreviated as Na`Vi ) is an ukranian E-Sports Clan that started out at the end 2009. It was formed from the strongest Players in Ukraine back then. Over the the years Na`Vi won several different big Tournaments, but were unable to win a Major yet.
