Astralis

On this page you will get some Information about the current No. 2 CSGO-Team in the World.

History of Astralis

Astralis is a danish E-Sport-Organisation which was founded in 2016. With a total of 4 Major wins Astralis is considered as the best CS:GO Team in History. 3 of these Major wins are back-to-back wins.

Most Important Tournament wins

2019
BLAST Pro Series: Global Final 2019 (Prize Money: $350 000, Final against Team Liquid)
StarLadder Berlin Major 2019 (Prize Money: $500 000, Final against AVANGAR)
Intel Extreme Masters XIII - Katowice Major 2019 (Prize Money: $500 000, Final against ENCE)

2018
ESL Pro League Season 8 - Finals (Prize Money: $250 000, Final against Team Liquid)
Esports Championship Series Season 6 - Finals (Prize Money: $250 000, Final against MIBR)
FACEIT Major: London 2018 (Prize Money: $500 000, Final against Natus Vincere)
ELEAGUE CS:GO Premier 2018 (Prize Money: $500 000, Final against Team Liquid)

2017
ELEAGUE Major: Atlanta 2017 (Prize Money: $500 000, Final against Virtus.Pro)
