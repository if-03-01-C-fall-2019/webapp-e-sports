On this page you will get some Information about the current No. 2 League-Team in the World.

History of G2 Esports

G2 Esports is a spanish E-Sport organisation based in Berlin. G2 Esports was founded in 2014. G2 Esports have been active in League of Legends sicne 2014. They are currently known as the best team in whole Europe
