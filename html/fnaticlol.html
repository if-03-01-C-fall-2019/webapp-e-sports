Fnatic

On this page you will get some Information about the current No. 4 League-Team in the World.

History of Fnatic

Fnatic is a british E-Sports organisation based in London. Fnatic is a relatively old organisation which was founded back in 2004. Fnatic entered the League of Legends scene in 2011
