T1

On this page you will get some Information about the current No. 1 League-Team in the World.

History of T1

T1 (formerly known SK Telecom T1) is a E-Sports team that was founded at the end of 2003. The first started out as a StarCraft Team. Since 2012 they are active also active in League of Legends. With three World Tournament wins they are the record holder right now.
